# Spatial Design Mentor

## English

Spatial Design Mentor is an early-stage architecture and spatial design assistant for students, designers, and educators.

It helps users move from site evidence to design thinking by reviewing project briefs, identifying missing information, reading site conditions, matching relevant architect methods, and supporting design critique and presentation structure.

This project is part of a broader Spatial AI direction. The long-term goal is to connect architectural design guidance with satellite imagery, contour maps, GIS data, climate information, site photographs, sketches, and other spatial evidence.

The current version focuses on the design mentoring framework:

- Project brief review
- Site and context analysis guidance
- Architect and design method matching
- Concept and strategy development
- Critique support
- Presentation and jury narrative support

Spatial Design Mentor is not intended to generate finished designs or imitate famous architects. It is intended to help users ask better design questions, build stronger spatial reasoning, and explain design decisions with evidence.

Future directions include:

- Satellite and aerial image interpretation
- Contour and slope analysis
- GIS and OpenStreetMap context reading
- Climate, sun, wind, flood, and ecological analysis
- Sketch, plan, and diagram understanding
- Automated site analysis diagrams
- Spatial AI assisted design review

## 中文

Spatial Design Mentor 是一个面向建筑、空间设计、景观、城市设计学生和设计者的早期设计导师项目。

它帮助使用者从场地证据出发，逐步完成任务书审查、信息缺失判断、场地条件分析、建筑师方法匹配、设计批评和汇报逻辑整理。

这个项目也是 Spatial AI 方向的一部分。长期目标是把建筑设计指导与卫星图、等高线图、GIS 数据、气候信息、场地照片、草图和其他空间证据连接起来。

当前版本主要关注设计导师框架：

- 项目任务书审查
- 场地与周边环境分析引导
- 建筑师与设计方法匹配
- 概念与策略推导
- 设计评图与修改建议
- 汇报、答辩和作品集叙事支持

Spatial Design Mentor 不是为了直接生成最终设计，也不是为了模仿某位著名建筑师的风格。它的目标是帮助使用者提出更好的设计问题，建立更清晰的空间推理，并用场地和功能证据解释设计决策。

未来计划包括：

- 卫星图和航拍图识别
- 等高线与坡度分析
- GIS 和 OpenStreetMap 场地语境读取
- 气候、日照、风、洪水和生态分析
- 草图、平面图和分析图理解
- 自动生成场地分析图
- Spatial AI 辅助设计评审
